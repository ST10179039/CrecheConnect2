
# Parent Media Quick Guide

## How to View Your Child's Photos and Videos

### Step 1: Open Media Tab
Tap the **Media** icon in the bottom navigation bar.

### Step 2: Browse Media
- Scroll through photos and videos in the grid
- If you have multiple children, use the filter buttons at the top to view media for a specific child
- See the count of photos, videos, and total media at the top

### Step 3: View Full Screen
Tap any photo or video to open it in full-screen mode.

### Step 4: Download Media
1. While viewing media in full screen, tap the **Download** button at the bottom
2. If this is your first time, you'll be asked to grant permission - tap **Allow**
3. Wait for the download to complete (you'll see a loading indicator)
4. A success message will appear when done
5. Find your downloaded media in your device's Photos/Gallery app

## Tips

### Filtering by Child
If you have multiple children enrolled:
- Tap **All Children** to see media for all your children
- Tap a specific child's name to see only their media

### Refreshing
Pull down on the screen to refresh and check for new media.

### Viewing Details
In full-screen mode, you can see:
- Caption (if added by teacher)
- Which child the media belongs to
- When it was uploaded

## Privacy & Security

- You can only view media of your own children
- All media is securely stored and transmitted
- Only teachers and administrators can upload media
- Media is only shared with parents of the children in the photos/videos
