**Option 1**: Tap notification in dashboard
- Opens full announcements screen
- Marks that announcement as read

**Option 2**: Open Announcements tab
- Automatically marks ALL announcements as read
- No need to tap individual items

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Will I get notified if the app is closed?
**A**: Currently, notifications only appear when the app is open. Push notifications for closed app are planned for a future update.

### Q: Can I delete announcements?
**A**: No, only admins can delete announcements. Parents can only view and mark as read.

### Q: How long do announcements stay visible?
**A**: All announcements remain visible indefinitely. You can scroll through the complete history in the Announcements tab.

### Q: What if I miss a notification?
**A**: Don't worry! All announcements are saved and accessible in the Announcements tab. You can view them anytime.

### Q: Can I search for old announcements?
**A**: Currently, announcements are sorted by date (newest first). Search functionality may be added in a future update.

### Q: Do I need internet to receive notifications?
**A**: Yes, real-time notifications require an active internet connection. When you reconnect, the app will automatically load any missed announcements.

## Troubleshooting

### Notifications Not Appearing?

1. **Check Internet Connection**
   - Ensure you have active WiFi or mobile data
   - Try pulling down to refresh

2. **Restart the App**
   - Close and reopen Cr√®cheConnect
   - Notifications should load automatically

3. **Check Announcements Tab**
   - Even if dashboard doesn't update, announcements are saved
   - Open Announcements tab to see all messages

### Notification Count Not Updating?

1. **Pull to Refresh**
   - Swipe down on dashboard to refresh
   - Counts should update immediately

2. **Open Announcements Tab**
   - This marks all as read
   - Return to dashboard to see updated count
